# Executive Summary — Amman Digital Market Analytics

## Top Findings

1.Consistent Revenue Growth: The market has successfully generated a total revenue of 48,701.50 JOD, showing a steady upward trend throughout the analyzed period.
2.Statistically Significant Category Performance: Differences in average order values across product categories are verified as real and not accidental, with a P-Value of 0.0000.
3.High-Demand Product Clusters: A small group of key products, led by Arabic Coffee Blend and Denim Jeans, drives the majority of sales volume.

## Supporting Data

Finding 1 Support: Total revenue calculated during Task 2 and the visual trend shown in kpi1_monthly_revenue.png.

Finding 2 Support: One-way ANOVA test results from Task 3 showing a P-Value < 0.05 and the distribution in kpi2_category_revenue.png.

Finding 3 Support: The "Top 5 Best-Selling Products" list from Task 2 and the horizontal bar chart in executive_dashboard.png.

## Recommendations

1.Strategic Category Investment: Since the statistical test confirms significant differences between categories, reallocate marketing budget toward high-performing segments like Electronics and Books to maximize ROI.
2.Inventory Safety Stock: Increase stock levels for top-selling items like the Arabic Coffee Blend (500g) to ensure zero "out-of-stock" days, as this is a primary volume driver.
3.Peak-Time Promotional Campaigns: Use the data from the Weekly Order Volume Activity chart to schedule flash sales and push notifications during the highest traffic weeks of the month.
